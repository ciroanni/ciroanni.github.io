Cooperative physics-based gameplay with active ragdolls.

The project was realized as part of the game design course at the Polytechnic of Turin, whose objective was to simulate the presentation of a video game prototype to a publisher. The course involved the creation of all the necessary elements, including a trailer, a pitch document and a working prototype. Within the group of 6 people, my contribution covered various aspects: I dealt with 3D modelling, teaser trailer animation, QA and, above all, programming, with a specific focus on character physics and the combat system.
