CampuSafeVR

A Virtual Reality training experience to learn emergency management in a university residence (Meta Quest 2).

Overview

CampuSafeVR is designed to help users practice emergency procedures within a virtual university residence. The experience aims to increase preparedness by simulating realistic emergency scenarios and offering interactive tasks that teach correct responses.

Implementation

The project was implemented in Unity with VR interactions tailored for Meta Quest 2. Systems include object interaction, scenario sequencing, and basic scoring/feedback mechanics to guide users through emergency workflows.

Role & Contributions

My contributions included coding the emergency scenarios, creating 3D models, and implementing object interactions. The team consisted of Ciro, Barbara, Rob and Alessandro.

Notes

Developed for the Virtual Reality exam at Politecnico di Torino (Prof. Andrea Bottino & Prof. Francesco Strada). The itch.io link hosts the build and additional project information.
