CampuSafeVR

  • Keywords: Unity, C#, VR, Meta Quest 2
  • Itch.io

CampuSafeVR is a Virtual Reality experience that allows users to learn how to manage emergencies within a virtual university residence, preparing them for real-life scenarios.

This project was made as part of the exam of Virtual Reality at Politecnico di Torino for the academic year 2023-2024, held by Professors Andrea Bottino and Francesco Strada. Team is Ciro, Barbara, Rob, Alessandro.

My contribution to the project was the coding of the emergency scenarios, the creation of the 3D models and the implementation of the interactions with the objects in the scene.
