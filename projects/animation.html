3D Commercial AD

Short animated commercial produced for the Computer Animation course — modelling, lighting and animation using Blender.

Quick info

Reference
Keywords
Blender, 3D Modelling, Computer Animation

Project

This project was completed as part of the Computer Animation course. The objective was to produce a short commercial-style animation. The team of four members split responsibilities by scene — my contributions cover the shots at 0:10–0:17, 0:24–0:27 and 0:30–0:37, focusing on modelling, lighting and animation of those sequences.

Role & Contributions

Led modelling and animation for selected scenes, created props and set dressing, adjusted lighting and rendered final frames. Collaborated on shot composition and post-processing to ensure visual consistency across the film.

Techniques

Modelling • Lighting • Animation

Workflow included low-to-high poly modelling, UVs and texturing, rigging for animated elements, keyframe animation and physically based lighting setups. Final renders assembled and color-graded in post.

Notes

Project completed for academic purposes. Full team credits appear in the video description. The work demonstrates practical pipeline experience in Blender for short-form advertising content.
