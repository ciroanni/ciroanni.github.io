3D Commercial AD

The project was realized as part of the computer animation course at the Polytechnic of Turin, whose objective was to reproduce a short animated commercial ad. My main contributions included the scene from 0:10 to 0:17, the scene from 0:24 to 0:27 and the last one from 0:30 to 0:37.
